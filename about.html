About the Periodic Table Chatbot

Welcome to the Periodic Table Chatbot, a handy tool for exploring information about chemical elements. This chatbot is designed to provide you with quick and easy access to data from the periodic table.

How It Works: The chatbot allows you to upload a CSV file containing periodic table data and then query the elements. You can ask questions about elements, such as their name, atomic number, symbol, and more. The chatbot will retrieve the relevant data from the CSV file and provide you with the answers you're looking for.

Specifications: The chatbot uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a user-friendly interface. It's designed to be responsive and easy to use. You can upload your own periodic table data in CSV format to make it work for your specific needs.

We hope you find the Periodic Table Chatbot helpful for your educational or research needs. If you have any feedback or suggestions for improvement, please feel free to get in touch with us. Enjoy exploring the world of chemical elements!
