Instructions

How to Query !

Input any element name to get normal details.

  1. For the number of neutrons, use: "number of neutrons in [Element Name]."

  2. To find the symbol, use: "What is the symbol of [Element Name]."

  3. For atomic mass, use: "What is the atomic mass of [Element Name]."

  4. To determine the number of protons, use: "Number of protons in [Element Name]."

  5. For electrons, use: "Number of electrons in [Element Name]."

  6. To get the period, use: "What is the period of [Element Name]."

  7. To find the group, use: "What is the group of [Element Name]."

  8. To check the phase, use: "What is the phase of [Element Name]."

  9. For radioactivity, use: "Radioactivity of [Element Name]."

  10. To check natural occurrence, use: "Natural or not [Element Name]."

  11. To find boiling point, use: "What is the boiling point of [Element Name]."

  12. To find isotopes, use: "Number of isotopes in [Element Name]."

  13. To find the discoverer, use: "Who discovered [Element Name]."

  14. For the discovery year, use: "Discovery year of [Element Name]."

  15. To get specific heat, use: "Specific heat of [Element Name]."

  16. For metallicity, use: "Metal or not [Element Name]."

  17. To check nonmetallicity, use: "Nonmetal or not [Element Name]."

  18. To check metalloid nature, use: "Metalloid or not [Element Name]."

  19. For the element type, use: "What is the type of [Element Name]."

  20. To get atomic radius, use: "What is the Atomic Radius of [Element Name]."

  21. For electronegativity, use: "What is the Electronegativity of [Element Name]."

  22. To find first ionization, use: "What is the First Ionization of [Element Name]."

  23. To check density, use: "What is the Density of [Element Name]."

  24. To find melting point, use: "What is the Melting Point of [Element Name]."

  25. To get the number of shells, use: "Number of Shells in [Element Name]."

  26. For valence electrons, use: "Number of Valence in [Element Name]."


    27. Please ensure you replace [Element Name] with the name of the element you want to inquire about in each query.
    Be more cautious about characters as it is character sensitive.
